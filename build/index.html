ELECTROKNIT

Welcome to a world of knit!

Electroknit is a knit company founded by the Swedish designer Hanna Rubin.

Inspired by the ancient technique of knitting we want to challenge the idea of what a knitted piece can be and taking it in to new contexts by using unexpected materials and adding functions to the textile.

Our playful pieces are carefully made with many hours spent in the process of designing and making. We also create custom made pieces, knit samples and are always looking for new inspiring collaborations with in all areas of the creative industry.
